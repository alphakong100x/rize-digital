Frequently Asked Questions

How does Rize Digital's exclusive partnership model work?

We partner with only one home service business per specific industry (e.g., roofing, plumbing) within a defined geographic service area. This ensures our team is 100% focused on your success and eliminates any conflict of interest. Your growth is our sole priority in your market.

What is the typical onboarding timeline for new clients?

Our onboarding process is thorough yet efficient. It typically involves: Weeks 1-2: Deep Dive Discovery & Strategy. Weeks 3-4: Core Asset Build (website, GHL setup, ad accounts). Week 5: Campaign Launch & Initial Optimization. SEO results build progressively, often seeing significant traction in 4-6 months, while PPC can generate leads almost immediately.

What is the minimum contract length for Rize Digital's services?

To achieve meaningful, sustainable regional dominance, our partnerships require a commitment. Standard minimum contract lengths are 12 months for Regional/National Dominance packages and 6 months for Local Market Leader packages. This allows ample time to implement, optimize, and scale our comprehensive strategies for maximum ROI.

What types of home service businesses do you work with?

We specialize in partnering with ambitious home service businesses generating $1M-$20M+ in annual revenue, including (but not limited to) HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, landscaping, tree care, pest control, cleaning services, restoration, remodeling, fencing, excavation, and concrete contractors who are ready to invest in significant growth (typically $4k-$20k+/month in marketing).

How do you measure and report on ROI?

We believe in radical transparency. You'll have access to a custom dashboard (often within GoHighLevel) tracking key performance indicators (KPIs) like leads generated, cost per lead (CPL), conversion rates, website traffic, search rankings, and ultimately, return on ad spend (ROAS) and overall marketing ROI. We provide regular reports and strategy calls to review performance and plan next steps.
