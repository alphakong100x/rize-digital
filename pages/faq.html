Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about how Rize Digital can help your home service business achieve regional dominance? Find answers to common inquiries about our services, process, and partnership model.

Your Questions, Answered

What types of home service businesses does Rize Digital specialize in?

Rize Digital specializes in partnering with ambitious home service businesses typically generating $1M-$20M+ in annual revenue. This includes, but is not limited to, HVAC contractors, plumbers, electricians, roofers, landscapers, tree care services, pest control companies, cleaning services, restoration companies, remodelers, fencing contractors, excavation services, and concrete contractors. If you're a home service business ready to invest seriously in growth (typically $4k-$20k+/month in marketing), we're likely a good fit.

How does Rize Digital's exclusive partnership model work?

Our exclusive partnership model means we work with only ONE home service business per specific industry (e.g., one roofer, one plumber) within a defined geographic service area. This eliminates any conflict of interest and ensures that 100% of our strategic efforts, local market insights, and competitive advantages are dedicated to your success in that territory. When you partner with us, we turn away your direct competitors in your market.

What is the typical onboarding process and timeline?

Our onboarding is thorough to ensure success. It generally follows this timeline: Weeks 1-2: Deep Dive Discovery (audits, competitor analysis, ICP clarification, goal setting). Weeks 3-4: Strategic Planning & Core Asset Build (website framework, GHL setup, ad account structuring). Week 5: Campaign Launch & Initial Optimization. While PPC campaigns can start generating leads quickly, SEO is a long-term strategy, typically showing significant traction and organic results within 4-6 months, with continued growth thereafter.

What is the minimum contract length for your services?

To achieve meaningful and sustainable regional dominance, our partnerships require a commitment that allows our comprehensive strategies to take full effect. Our standard minimum contract length is 12 months for Regional/National Dominance packages and 6 months for Local Market Leader packages. This duration ensures we have adequate time to implement, optimize, track, and scale our full-stack solutions for maximum ROI for your business.

How do you measure and report on ROI?

We believe in radical transparency and data-driven results. You will have access to a custom performance dashboard (often within GoHighLevel) that tracks key performance indicators (KPIs) in real-time. These include leads generated, cost per lead (CPL), conversion rates, website traffic, search engine rankings, click-through rates, and ultimately, return on ad spend (ROAS) and overall marketing ROI. We also provide regular, detailed reports and conduct strategy calls to review performance, discuss insights, and collaboratively plan next steps.

What makes Rize Digital different from other marketing agencies?

Several key factors differentiate Rize Digital: 1) Our Exclusive Partnership Model: We focus all our energy on one client per industry/territory. 2) Specialization in Home Services: We have deep expertise in what works for contractors. 3) Full-Stack Approach: We integrate SEO, PPC, Web Dev, AI, and Email into a cohesive strategy. 4) Technology-Driven: We leverage advanced tools like Oxygen Builder, ACF, GoHighLevel, and AI. 5) Focus on Regional Dominance: We're not just about leads; we're about making you the #1 choice in your multi-state service area.
