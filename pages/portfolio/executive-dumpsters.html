Client Overview

Client: Executive Dumpsters

Industry: Dumpster Rental Services

Region: Eastern North Carolina

Executive Dumpsters aimed to become the premier dumpster rental provider across Eastern North Carolina. However, they were struggling with high cost-per-click (CPC) in their Google Ads campaigns and limited organic visibility beyond their immediate home city.

The Challenge: High Ad Costs and Limited Organic Reach

Executive Dumpsters faced significant hurdles in achieving their regional dominance goals:

  • Unsustainable Google Ads Costs: High CPCs were making it difficult to achieve a positive ROI on their paid advertising.
  • Poor Ad Performance: Existing Responsive Search Ads (RSAs) had low Quality Scores and weren't effectively qualifying leads.
  • Localized Organic Visibility: Their website ranked well in their home city but struggled to gain traction in surrounding counties and service areas.
  • Intense Competition: The dumpster rental market is competitive, with many local and regional players.

Our Solution: Integrated SEO Content Hubs & PPC Optimization

Rize Digital developed a comprehensive strategy to tackle both paid and organic challenges:

  1. PPC Campaign Overhaul & RSA Optimization:
    • Conducted a deep analysis of existing campaigns to identify inefficiencies.
    • Rewrote all Responsive Search Ad (RSA) copy, focusing on strong calls-to-action, benefit-driven messaging, and keyword relevance to improve Quality Scores.
    • Refined keyword targeting to focus on high-intent, long-tail keywords with lower competition.
    • Implemented smarter bidding strategies and optimized campaign structures for better budget allocation.
  2. SEO Content Hub Development for Regional Dominance:
    • Identified key counties and service areas for expansion based on search volume and competitive landscape.
    • Created comprehensive "content hubs" for each target region, including dedicated service area pages optimized for local keywords (e.g., "dumpster rental [City Name]").
    • Developed supporting blog content and resources relevant to dumpster rental needs in Eastern NC, interlinking strategically with hub pages.
    • Focused on technical SEO best practices to ensure all new content was easily discoverable and crawlable by search engines.
    • Built high-quality local citations and backlinks to boost the authority of regional pages.

This synergistic approach aimed to reduce reliance on high-cost PPC over time by building a strong organic foundation, while simultaneously making existing PPC campaigns more efficient and effective.

The Results: Regional Market Leadership & Improved ROI

The integrated strategy yielded remarkable results for Executive Dumpsters:

  • #1 Organic Ranking in 7 Target Counties: SEO content hubs successfully propelled Executive Dumpsters to the top of search results in their desired expansion areas.
  • 41% Lower Cost Per Click (CPC): Optimization of Google Ads campaigns significantly reduced ad spend while improving lead quality.
  • 189% Increase in Organic Website Traffic: The new content strategy dramatically boosted visibility and organic traffic from target regions.
  • Increased Qualified Lead Flow: Both PPC and organic channels began delivering a higher volume of qualified leads ready to book services.
  • Established Regional Authority: Executive Dumpsters became recognized as a leading provider across Eastern North Carolina.
