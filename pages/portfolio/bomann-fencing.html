Client Overview

Client: Bomann Fencing

Industry: Agricultural & Residential Fencing

Region: Georgia, USA

Bomann Fencing is a respected fencing contractor specializing in agricultural and residential projects. Despite their quality workmanship, they were experiencing disappointing results from their previous marketing agency, with low lead quality from Google Ads and minimal organic visibility.

The Challenge: Poor Lead Quality and Low Online Visibility

Bomann Fencing faced several critical marketing challenges:

  • Ineffective Google Ads: Campaigns were generating clicks but few qualified leads, resulting in wasted ad spend.
  • Minimal Organic Presence: The business had poor rankings in local search results and the Google Map Pack.
  • Lack of Trust in Agencies: Previous negative experiences made them skeptical of marketing partners.
  • Stagnant Growth: The lack of effective marketing was hindering their ability to grow and reach new customers.

Our Solution: Holistic Local Marketing Overhaul

Rize Digital implemented a multi-faceted strategy focused on improving local visibility and lead quality:

  1. Google Business Profile (GBP) Optimization: We conducted a thorough optimization of Bomann Fencing's GBP listing, including:
    • Ensuring complete and accurate business information.
    • Optimizing service categories and service areas.
    • Regularly publishing GBP posts with relevant updates and offers.
    • Implementing a strategy to encourage and manage customer reviews.
  2. Strategic PPC Campaign Restructure: We overhauled their Google Ads campaigns by:
    • Conducting fresh keyword research focused on high-intent, local search terms.
    • Rewriting ad copy to improve relevance, qualification, and click-through rates.
    • Restructuring campaigns for better targeting and budget allocation.
    • Implementing robust conversion tracking to measure true ROI.
  3. AI-Powered Lead Management: Integrated AI chatbots and automated follow-up sequences via GoHighLevel to ensure rapid response to inquiries and consistent lead nurturing.
  4. Local SEO Enhancements: Implemented foundational on-page and local SEO tactics to improve organic rankings for key service terms within their specific geographic areas.

This comprehensive approach aimed to not only increase visibility but also attract higher-quality leads more likely to convert into paying customers.

The Results: Dramatic Turnaround and Renewed Confidence

The strategic overhaul delivered significant improvements for Bomann Fencing:

  • 4x Increase in Conversion Rate: The combination of better ad targeting and optimized local presence dramatically improved lead quality and conversion rates.
  • 122% More Map Pack Clicks: Enhanced GBP visibility led to a surge in local customers finding them via Google Maps.
  • 28 New 5-Star Reviews: Proactive review management strategy boosted their online reputation and social proof.
  • #1 Rankings Secured: Achieved top organic rankings across their primary service area for key search terms.
  • Restored Client Confidence: The tangible results and transparent approach rebuilt trust and demonstrated the power of effective digital marketing.
