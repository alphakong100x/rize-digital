Wasting Money on Google Ads That Don't Convert?

Many home service businesses pour money into Google Ads with little to show for it. Unfocused campaigns, poor keyword targeting, and weak ad copy lead to high costs per lead (CPL) and minimal ROI. It's time to transform your PPC from an expense into a predictable lead generation machine.

The Pitfalls of Ineffective PPC Management:

  • Sky-high CPLs eating into your profit margins.
  • Attracting unqualified clicks that don't turn into actual jobs.
  • Low Quality Scores leading to higher ad costs and poor visibility.
  • Lack of transparency and clear reporting on campaign performance.

The Rize Digital "Jump-Start & Redeploy" PPC Methodology

Our approach to Google Ads is strategic, data-driven, and relentlessly focused on maximizing your ROI. We combine rapid campaign deployment with continuous optimization and strategic scaling.

1. Deep Dive & Strategy

We start with a comprehensive audit of your existing accounts (if any), competitor analysis, and in-depth keyword research to identify high-intent search terms specific to your services and target regions.

2. Campaign Architecture & Launch

We build tightly-themed ad groups, compelling ad copy, and high-converting landing pages. Campaigns are structured for precision geo-targeting, ensuring your ads reach the right customers in your specific service areas.

3. Continuous Optimization (CRO)

Launch is just the beginning. We constantly monitor campaign performance, A/B test ad copy and landing pages, refine keyword targeting, manage bids, and implement negative keywords to maximize conversions and minimize wasted spend.

4. Scale & Redeploy

As we achieve success and optimal CPLs in core markets, we strategically redeploy winning formulas to expand your reach into new service areas or target new customer segments, fueling sustainable regional growth.
