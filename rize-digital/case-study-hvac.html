Case Study: HVAC Contractor

How we helped a local HVAC contractor achieve a 250% increase in qualified leads

The Challenge

Our client, a local HVAC contractor, was struggling with inconsistent lead flow and high customer acquisition costs. Despite being in business for over 10 years with excellent customer reviews, they were heavily reliant on word-of-mouth referrals and expensive Yellow Pages advertising.

The Solution

We developed a comprehensive digital marketing strategy that included local SEO optimization, Google Ads campaigns, and lead generation systems. Our approach focused on capturing high-intent customers actively searching for HVAC services in their area.

The Results

Within 6 months, our client achieved remarkable results: a 250% increase in qualified leads, 40% reduction in cost-per-lead, and a 185% increase in monthly revenue. The Google Ads campaigns generated an average ROAS of 4.2:1, while local SEO efforts resulted in first-page rankings for 15 target keywords.

Transforming home service businesses through innovative digital marketing solutions.
